How to configure a proxy on your mobile phone?

To set up a proxy on your device, you have two options depending on your network connection – either mobile cellular or Wi-Fi. Here is how to configure a proxy on your device:

  • Open Settings on your mobile phone.
  • Go to Wi-Fi or Network Settings, depending on your device then select the Wi-Fi network you are using.
  • Long-press on the network name and choose "Modify Network" or "Advanced Settings."
  • In the Proxy settings section, select "Manual" or "Proxy."
  • Enter the proxy server address and port provided by your proxy service.
  • Save your settings and exit the menu.
  • Finally, disconnect and reconnect to the Wi-Fi network to apply the changes.
