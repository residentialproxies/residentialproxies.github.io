What are Rotating Proxies?

Rotating proxies are a type of proxy server that automatically changes the IP address assigned to a user's connection at regular intervals or after each request. This rotation of IP addresses helps users maintain anonymity, bypass IP-based restrictions, and avoid detection when engaging in activities such as web scraping, data mining, or accessing geo-restricted content.

Rotating proxies typically operate by cycling through a pool of IP addresses, ensuring that users appear to be connecting from different locations and devices, thereby enhancing privacy and evading detection.
