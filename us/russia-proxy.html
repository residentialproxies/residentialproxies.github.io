What are the differences between a Russia Socks5 proxy and an HTTP(s) proxy?

A Socks5 proxy is a more general proxy that can handle various types of traffic, including TCP and UDP, and offers more security features like authentication. An HTTP(s) proxy, on the other hand, is specifically designed for web traffic and supports HTTPS for encrypted traffic. Socks5 proxies are often preferred for their versatility and security.
